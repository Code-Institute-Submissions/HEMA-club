Frequently Asked Questions

  • What equipment do I need to train?

    For general training we request that you have workout clothing that colors the whole body (long-sleeved shirt, long-sleeved pants, light gloves, training shoes). This is to help prevent accidental cuts from training weapons.


    For full-contact sparring please speak with your instuctor for the required equipment for your specific course.

  • Is there an age limit?

    For those under 18 we offer various youth courses. For full-contact sparring you must be over 18.

  • Do I need to buy equipment?

    The club offers equimpent use during courses. For full-contact sparring there is an option to rent equipment from the club.

    If you would like to purchase your own equipment please contact your instructor.
